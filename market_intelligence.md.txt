## 4. Market Intelligence Agent

**Prompt Template:**
```
Analyze market data:
{market_data}
Summarize competitive trends and strategic insights.
```
**Placeholders:** `{market_data}`

**Example Input:**
```
market_data = "Q2 earnings of top 5 EV makers."
```
**Example Output:**
```
Tesla leads with 12% growth. BYD outpaces in China. Rivian underperforms YoY.
```
**Test Criteria:**
- Insight accuracy
- Business relevance
- Sentiment correctness
